Key Insights
Winter admission surge
Nov–Jan consistently show peak admissions, likely driven by cold weather-induced cardiovascular stress and elevated winter pollution levels.
Renal & cardiac highest risk
AKI (16.2%) and CKD (14.4%) carry the highest mortality. STEMI at 9.4% demands rapid intervention protocols to reduce fatality.
PM2.5/PM10 dominate AQI
Particulate matter drives 93% of poor-AQI days. Avg PM2.5 of 112 μg/m³ far exceeds WHO safe limits, correlating with elevated cardio admissions.
